Keeling Curve

This Module presents the Keeling Curve, the atmospheric concentration of carbon dioxide over time. The curve is named after atmospheric scientist Charles David Keeling. It utilizes data from NOAA's Earth Systems Research Laboratory collected from their Mauna Loa Observatory. The dynamic graph appears above the collection site on Mauna Loa. A GeoScope layer with the OnTerra Bing Maps Aerial data truncated to level 10 is recommended presenting for this module.

The module will appear as "cherry blossoms" in the object tree, under Solar System/Planets/Earth. The variable KeelingCurveYearPlot is used to animate the graph. The data span is from 1974-2016.
