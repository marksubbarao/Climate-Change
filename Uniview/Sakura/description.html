Sakura

A module indicating how the cherry blossom blooming time in Kyoto gives us a climate estimator going back over 1000 years. .

The module will appear as "Global Temperature Anomoly" in the object tree, under Solar System/Planets/Earth. The variable sakuraYearPlot is used to animate the graph. The data span is from 850-1996.

Dependencies

Earth

Created by

Mark SubbaRao and Takumi Inoue,
based on data from:
Aono and Kazui (2008; International Journal of Climatology, 28, 905-914) and Aono (2012; Chikyu Kankyo (Global Environment), 17, 21-29) for citation (Chikyu Kankyo in Japanese means the Global Environment. This paper was written in Japanese).
